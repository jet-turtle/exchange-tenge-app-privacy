Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Exchange Tenge.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. The app is designed to provide exchange rate information without requiring any user registration.

2. Local Data Storage

The app uses Jetpack DataStore to save your UI preferences locally on your device, such as selected language (kk, ru, en)

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The app fetches data from the National Bank of Kazakhstan (nationalbank.kz). Please note that when the app requests XML data, your IP address may be visible to their servers as part of a standard HTTP request.

We may use Google Play Services for app distribution and updates. You can find their policy here: Google Privacy & Terms.

4. Security

We value your trust. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of your personal identity being compromised through the app.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
